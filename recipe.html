Title: Lasagna
Category: Lunch, Dinner
Servings: 8
Time Length: 1 Hr. 15 Min.
Ingredients:
  • 1 lb. lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 15 oz. ricotta
  • 10 oz. mozzarella
Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cook beef in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in pasta sauce.
  3. Layer meat sauce, lasagna noodles, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.
